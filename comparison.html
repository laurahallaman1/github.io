About the Weather Project

For this project, cities within a certain distance from the equator were compared on four different factors: Cloudiness, Humidity, Temperature, and Wind. Each was graphed against the city's latitude to determine whether a correlation exists between this distance from the equator, and these 4 weather factors. Temperature was most strongly correllated, with Humidity coming in second, and Cloudiness and Wind being less correllated. All weather data was sourced from OpenWeatherAPI, a respected source of weather and climate data.
